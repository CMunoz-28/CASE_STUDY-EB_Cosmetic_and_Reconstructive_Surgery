# Content Migration

## Overview
This folder documents the **content migration planning** process for the East Bay Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery website redesign.

The objective was to audit legacy site content, determine what should be retained, updated, merged, or retired, and prepare the content for migration into a modern CMS while preserving SEO equity.

---

## Key Artifact
- **Content_Inventory.xlsx**
  - Page-level inventory of existing site content
  - URL status, content type, and migration recommendations
  - Notes on SEO relevance and consolidation opportunities

---

## Key Considerations
- Preservation of high-performing SEO pages  
- Removal of outdated or redundant content  
- Alignment with new site architecture and service segmentation  
- Support for HIPAA-aware content handling
