# Strategy Documents

## Overview
This folder contains **strategy and planning documentation** developed to guide the digital transformation of East Bay Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery.

Materials were created to support **executive decision-making**, platform selection, and implementation planning.

---

## Key Documents
- **Website Audit Report**
  - Technical SEO findings and UX issues
  - Performance, structure, and usability insights

- **Website Redesign Opportunities**
  - Strategic recommendations for improving user experience and conversion flow

- **CMS Platform Comparison Proposal**
  - Evaluation of CMS options with emphasis on SEO scalability and HIPAA compatibility

- **Project Timelines**
  - Implementation roadmap outlining phases, dependencies, and milestones

---

## Strategic Focus Areas
- Brand repositioning and service segmentation
- SEO remediation and technical improvements
- CMS scalability and long-term maintainability
- HIPAA-aware lead generation planning

---

## Outcome
These documents supported:
- CMS selection (WordPress recommendation)
- Stakeholder alignment and approval
- Structured execution of the redesign initiative
